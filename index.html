Two people will argue on their views and opinions of colour tattoos vs black and white ink. We will go through many factors including during the tattooing process, looks, expression, and healing

Izzu's Argument on Colour Tattoos

COLOUR TATTOOS

Colour tattoos are known to express and have a much greater statement than black and white ink. They draw more attention to the skin and image because of the flashy colours and intense contrast. I think colour tattoos are much better than black and white tattoos simply because they look cooler. It draws attention and tells a story, where black and white tattoos might not get the image across as easy.

On the other hand, colour tattoos are more known to fade quicker and take longer to get done. This is due to the complexity of colour tattoos.
